## â“ Common Questions

### Q: Why do I need to verify my email?
**A:** Security! This ensures you own the email address and prevents spam accounts.

### Q: I didn't get the verification email. What do I do?
**A:** 
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Wait 1-2 minutes (sometimes emails are delayed)
3. Make sure you entered the correct email
4. Try signing up again with a different email

### Q: Can I sign in without signing up first?
**A:** No! You must create an account first. That's how authentication works.

### Q: I keep getting "Couldn't find your account"
**A:** You're trying to sign in before creating an account. Click "Don't have an account? Sign Up" at the bottom.

### Q: Is the "Clerk development keys" warning an error?
**A:** No! That's just an informational message. It appears for all developers and doesn't affect functionality.

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### Error: "Couldn't find your account"
**Cause:** Trying to sign in without signing up first  
**Solution:** Use the Sign Up form instead

### Error: "Email address must be a valid email"
**Cause:** Invalid email format  
**Solution:** Make sure your email has @ and a domain (e.g., @gmail.com)

### Error: "Password must be at least 8 characters"
**Cause:** Password too short  
**Solution:** Use at least 8 characters

### Error: "Invalid verification code"
**Cause:** Wrong code entered  
**Solution:** Double-check the code in your email
